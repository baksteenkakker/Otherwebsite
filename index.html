Surfer Dude Dance Party

Bring the sun, fun and beach in to your room and arcade halls with Surfer Dude Dance Party!

There can only be one king of the waves. Dude, surfer Dude is the king. He loves surfing and believes that surfing is living. He loves dancing as well, and likes to show his moves on big beach parties. Will you help Dude practice his dance skills while surfing?

Simple and innovative

The arcade game Surfer Dude Dance Party has a simple graphic style, which fits the arcade theme. This makes it appealing for a broad range of people. This game features smooth animations, making this game better than the average arcade game.

Easy and interesting

The controls for the pc version are WASD and the arrow keys, this makes it easy to get the hang of it. When playing the arcade game (which we highly recommend), you use a surfboard as controller. YOu stand on the surfboard and move it around in order to do the combos.

The team

BeNJAMIN studios is an alternative and small game studio. The team is hand picked to create the most fun, creative and highly skilled team that exists. The speciality of BeNJAMIN Studios is the creation of arcade games.
